Gosia Vitale

Counsellor & Psychotherapist in Brighton and Hove

At different stages in our lives, we encounter difficulties and issues that prevent us from fully enjoying and appreciating life. This can be anything from anxiety to the loss of a person (through death or end of a relationship), to deeper emotional issues such as; low self-esteem, inability to form relationships, or even repetitive behaviour.

Other times, we are unable to explain what is troubling us - we just know that something is.

The key to an effective outcome is enabling my clients to feel comfortable in a safe and trustworthy environment, in which genuine interest, empathy and attentive listening and understanding takes place. I work on the basis of complete trust and confidentiality adopting the BACP's ethical guidelines and confidentiality policies.

I provide a safe and non-judgmental space for my clients to explore how they feel and help them make sense of what they are going through.

Training & Qualifications

I am a qualified counsellor and psychotherapist, registered with the British Association for Counselling & Psychotherapy (BACP), and a graduate member of the British Psychological Society (BPS).

As every person is different, with different experiences and difficulties, there is no 'one size fits all' in therapy. By listening to my clients it is more than likely to use a combination of different methods and approaches within the therapy sessions to resolve issues, ranging from psychodynamic, mindfulness, narrative to CBT, to mention a few, as well as short term and long term therapy.
